Walk This Way

Song line: Cause she knew what was she was doin', When she told me how to walk this way! She told me to walk this way, talk this way.

Song: Walk This Way (This Bird Has Flown), released 1975

Artist: Aerosmith (American)

Lesson Link: reported speech

Notes:Aerosmith's quitarist, Joe Perry created the famous quitar riff for this song and the band developed the track's melody and sequence. However, it took the singer, Steven Tyler, so long to come up with the lyrics that they actually considered dumping the track. When Tyler had finally written the lyrics and entered the studio to record the song, he realized he had left the lyrics in a taxi! He wrote new lyrics on the wall of the studio, as he didn't have any paper with him, and the band finally recorded the song that became a huge hit.
