Song line: Why do I find it hard to write the next line? Oh, I want the truth to be said, I know this much is true.

Song: True, released in 1983.

Artist: Spandau Ballet (British)

Lesson Link: facts and opinions.

Notes: According to Spandau Ballet's guitarist Gary Kemp, he wrote the lyrics of this song inspired by a crush he had on singer and star Clare Grogan. The musician said that, despite the fact that his feelings were not reciprocated and their relationship was only platonic, he felt this was enough to write a song about.
