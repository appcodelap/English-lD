Another Brick in the Wall

Song line: Hey, Teacher, leave them kids alone! All in all you’re just another brick in the wall.

Song: Another Brick in the Wall, released in 1979

Artist: Pink Floyd (British)

Lesson Link: education

Notes: The song written by Pink Floyd is a reflection of Roger Water’s views on formal education. Waters hated his grammar school teachers who demanded total control, were dedicated to keeping students under control and treated students more like parts in a machine than as people with ideas. Waters thought that school presented a series of emotional barriers the singer had to build around himself to survive at school.
